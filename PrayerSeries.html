 "Prayer Series"  with Fr. John Catoir
Giving Yourself to God in Prayer is a multi-part video series intended to be used by individuals
or groups for retreat or private reflection. We invite you to learn how to relax in the Lord's
presence -- any time, any place -- "just as I am!"  The original video was produced and  offered
by The Christophers. It has been re-edited into smaller segments and used with permission.
