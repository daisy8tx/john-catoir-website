"Prayer to Live Joyfully"
Lord, at times I am overcome with joy!
The world seems such a glorious place and
all the cares of life seem far away.
Thank you for giving us a glimpse of eternity.

I know that such a grand feeling is sure to pass
and that I will be once again be confronted with
the problems and worries of daily life.

Let me realize that true joy never fades,
for it is the gift of Your Spirit made
possible by the saving action of Jesus.
Keep me in Your grace so that I may never
lose that inner virtue of joy!

Excerpt from "Tranformation in Christ" by Fr. John Catoir, JCD (CLICK ABOVE FOR FULL ARTICLE)

According to Father Thomas Radcliff, a former Superior General of the Dominican
Order, “This is what the phrase transformation in Christ means: We become, by
God’s grace, what others need us to be, namely, a ‘living Presence.’
We are no longer our natural selves.”

The word transformation is defined as ‘a thorough change in one’s manner and
appearance’. Becoming more Christlike is a matter of acting with kindness, mercy
and justice. As these acts become habitual, they form one’s character and destiny.

direct ascent to the mount of joy . . .
"Christ came to bring joy. . . joy is
