Fr. John Catoir - Articles from the 1980's
Click each Title below to view that article in "full screen" . . .  

Most were first published as part of his nationally-syndicated column appearing in
Catholic Newspapers during his term as Director of The Christophers, New York,
NY. The syndicated column continued, though, even in retirement until his last one
which was completed in October and published in November 2019. These have
been scanned from the originals, in the order placed in his scrapbooks through the
years. On many of the earliest ones -- no publication date was retained. If you wish
to come back to this page to view more articles, hit the TOP LEFT ARROW!
NOTE: We are trying to compile all the writings of Fr. Catoir in one
central depository. If you should have an older article, column or
other document by Fr. Catoir that is not already listed in The Archives,
please consider it sharing it with us for possible inclusion
on this website.
